How Agents Work Together

The system uses a sophisticated multi-agent workflow to ensure comprehensive and unbiased evaluation:

1
Multi-Agent Parallel Analysis

Submission Evaluator Agent: Evaluates each submission against criteria using specialized tools to access detailed submission information.

Criteria Analyzer Agent: Analyzes evaluation criteria to establish consistent assessment standards.

Feasibility Agent: Assesses the practical viability and implementation aspects of each submission.

Impact Agent: Evaluates potential impact, benefits, and long-term effectiveness of submissions.

2
Coordination & Aggregation

User Proxy Agent: Waits for all 4 research agents to complete their analysis, then coordinates and aggregates their findings before passing consolidated results to the summary stage.

3
Final Synthesis & Selection

Summary & Selection Agent: Processes all evaluation data, creates a comprehensive report, compares submissions using detailed tools, and selects the best submission with detailed justification.
